Repeat Prescriptions Info

Repeat prescriptions are available to patients without consultation once the medication has been agreed to by your GP and you have undergone an up-to-date medication review.


If you wish to change your medication or dosage, please make an appointment to discuss this with your GP.

Medication Reviews

If taking regular medications, you should review these at least every 12 months with your GP/Consultant.


Questions to ask:

  • Is this medication still meeting my health needs?
  • Do I still need to take this/these medications?
  • Is the medicine list on my record accurate?

Important Notes

Where a consultation may be required: There are certain medications that can only be prescribed monthly and must be authorised by the attending GP. Examples include strong painkillers and sleeping tablets.


48 Hours notice is required for all repeat prescriptions.
