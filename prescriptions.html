Repeat Prescriptions

Repeat prescriptions are available for stable, long-term conditions where the medication has been authorised by your GP.


If you wish to change your medication or dosage, please make an appointment to discuss this with your GP first.

Medication Reviews

To ensure your safety, patients on regular medication should have a review at least every 12 months.


Your GP may request that you attend for a review before issuing further prescriptions.

Antibiotic Prescribing Policy

Please note: Antibiotics are not available on repeat prescription. They are used to treat acute bacterial infections and require a clinical assessment.

If you believe you require antibiotics, please contact reception to arrange a consultation.
