Medical Certification & Sick Notes

General Information

Work Absences

If you need a sick note for work, you may need to attend the surgery in person and be seen by a GP who can confirm and certify your illness.


Hospital Stays

If you have been an inpatient in Hospital, we require a discharge letter from the hospital confirming your stay before we can provide certification.


Social Welfare Certificates

If you have been absent from work for more than six days, you may need a continuance sick note (Social Welfare Certificate).

  • We will need your PPS number and the dates of your sick leave.
  • Please contact reception 48 hours before your certificate is due.
  • These must be collected from reception in person.

Letters & Reports

Letters and reports requested by patients will incur a charge depending on the complexity of the condition and the time required to complete them.

Request a Sick Note Online

You can request a sickness certificate by completing our online form.
